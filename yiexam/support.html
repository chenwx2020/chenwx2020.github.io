Frequently Asked Questions

Account

How do I create an account?
Open the App and tap "Sign Up". You can register with your email or use Sign in with Apple.

I forgot my password.
Tap "Forgot Password" on the login screen and follow the instructions to reset your password via email.

How do I delete my account?
Please contact us at support@caleexamapp.com and we will process your account deletion request.

Study Features

How many practice questions are available?
The App contains over 1,000 bilingual (English/Chinese) practice questions covering all major exam domains: Foundations of Medicine, Acupuncture & Point Location, Chinese Herbology, Biomedicine, and more.

What is spaced repetition?
Spaced repetition is a scientifically proven study method that schedules reviews at optimal intervals based on your performance. The App uses the FSRS-5 algorithm to help you retain information more effectively.

Can I study offline?
The App requires an internet connection to load questions and sync your progress.

Subscriptions

Is the App free?
Yes! During the launch period, all features are free to use. Premium subscriptions will be available in the future.

How do I manage my subscription?
Go to Settings on your iPhone > Apple ID > Subscriptions to view and manage your subscription.

How do I request a refund?
Refunds are handled by Apple. Visit https://reportaproblem.apple.com or contact Apple Support.

Community

How do I report inappropriate content?
Tap the "..." menu on any post or comment and select "Report". Our moderation team will review it promptly.

Can I create a study group?
Yes! Go to Community > Groups > Create Group. You can invite others to join via invite code.

Technical Issues

The App is not loading / showing errors.

  1. Check your internet connection
  2. Force close the App and reopen it
  3. Update to the latest version from the App Store
  4. If the issue persists, contact us at support@caleexamapp.com

I found an error in a question or explanation.
We appreciate your feedback! Please report it through the Community forum or email us at support@caleexamapp.com with the question details.
